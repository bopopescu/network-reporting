Welcome to MoPub!

Control your mobile ad inventory

Mobile ad serving made easy
Instantly switch between iAd, AdMob, AdSense and your own custom campaigns with MoPub's simple WebView based mobile ad server for iPhone and Android.

Lightweight, open source client code
No complicated SDK, just a few files hosted on Google Code to add to your project. Ads open within your application for a better user experience.

Tracking that matters
Quickly see how your ads are performing and easily monitor user engagement.

Things to Know Before You Start

To serve ads with MoPub, you'll need to set up your Inventory and your Campaigns.

  • Inventory: The places in your app that could show an advertisement. Register each of your apps and the Ad Units within each app that host an ad.
  • Campaigns: An ad strategy that could be an ad network, a direct sold ad campaign, or a promotional house ad. Each campaign also sets the targeting criteria, impression constraints and the ad units to appear in.
