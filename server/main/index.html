WebView-based Inventory Management

Easily switch between iAd, AdSense and your own custom campaigns with MoPub's simple WebView based mediation approach.

  • Lightweight, open source client code. No complicated SDK, just a few files hosted on Google Code to add to your project.
  • Trackable. Keep an eye on performance with multiple placements.
  • Flexible. Target house or directly sold campaigns to specific slices of your inventory, and easily switch between iAd and AdSense for backfill.

It's the simplest thing that could possibly work, while still giving you full control over your inventory.
